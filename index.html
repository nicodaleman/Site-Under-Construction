About

Nico Daleman is a musicologist and audio engineer interested in the history and development of music technology and its influence in the performance of classical and contemporary music. Together with Katarina Mijlkovich, Chair of the music theory department at New England Conservatory, Nico is developing a series of MaxMSP patches and applications aimed to the instruction of 20th century and contemporary music theory. Currently based in Berlin, Nico was actively involved in the Boston classical music scene, juggling between creating content for classical music start-ups, audio technician gigs at concerts and festivals, and music admin internships, landing at the classical-music-streaming-service IDAGIO as a Database Editor. His interest in music technology and classical music, along with his digital sound processing fascination, lead him pursue a Masters in Sound Studies in Sonic Arts at the Universität der Künste in Berlin, where is currently enrolled.
